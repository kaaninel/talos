## Platform Tiers

* Tier 1: Automated tests, high-priority fixes.
* Tier 2: Tested from time to time, medium-priority bugfixes.
* Tier 3: Not tested by core Talos team, community tested.

### Tier 1

* Metal
* AWS
* GCP
